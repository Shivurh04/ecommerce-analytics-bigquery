# Exploratory & Business Metrics Insights

## Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
• Orders data contains multiple statuses such as Completed, Cancelled, and Returned, indicating a full order lifecycle.
• The dataset spans multiple years, enabling reliable trend and seasonality analysis.
• Most orders are associated with valid user IDs, making customer-level analysis trustworthy.

## Order Volume & Trends
• The business processes a large number of orders, indicating a mature e-commerce operation.
• Order volumes vary across years, suggesting growth phases and potential seasonal patterns.

## Revenue & Average Order Value (AOV)
• Revenue is calculated at the item level, ensuring accurate measurement of customer spending.
• Average Order Value (AOV) reflects how much customers spend per order and is a key lever for revenue growth.
• Improving AOV through cross-sell or bundling strategies can increase revenue without increasing traffic.
