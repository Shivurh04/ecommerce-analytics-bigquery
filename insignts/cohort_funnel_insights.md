# Cohort & Funnel Insights

## Cohort Retention Analysis
• Customer retention drops significantly after the first purchase across most cohorts.
• Later cohorts do not show strong improvement in retention, indicating persistent engagement challenges.
• Improving post-purchase experience and onboarding could help increase repeat purchases.

## Funnel Conversion Analysis
• A large number of users view products, but a smaller portion proceed to add items to the cart.
• The biggest drop-off occurs between product views and cart actions.
• This suggests potential friction related to pricing, product information, or user experience.

## Business Recommendations
• Improve product discovery and clarity to encourage cart additions.
• Introduce targeted incentives to convert high-intent users.
• Strengthen retention strategies to improve long-term customer value.
